Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2025

Copy URL + Title is a lightweight Chrome extension that allows users to copy the current tab's URL or the tab's title and URL to the clipboard with a single click.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It operates entirely on your device and does not interact with any external servers.

Permissions

  • Tabs: Used solely to access the title and URL of the current tab for copying purposes.
  • Storage: Used to save your preference (copy URL only or copy title + URL) locally on your browser.

No Third-Party Access

Your data is never shared, sold, or sent to any third-party services. This extension runs entirely locally in your browser environment.

Changes to This Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, they will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to contact the developer via the support page on the Chrome Web Store.
